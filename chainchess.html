Overview

A level-based variant of Chess where you use the pieces you capture. Contains over 80 levels of increasing difficulty and a range of enemy abilities.

This project began as a prototype made in 2 days for a game jam. After receiving strong feedback about its potential, development continued on-and-off over weekends.

Key Achievements

Responsive UI

To provide accessibility to players on mobile, a portrait mode was added to the UI system. This involved refactoring the system to check for screen resolution changes.

Level Builder

By representing levels as data in a Scriptable Object, with the data consisting of piece positions/abilities and star score thresholds, development of levels could scale quickly.
