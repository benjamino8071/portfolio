Overview

A competitive online multiplayer game where players must pass and shoot a ball to score goals in a gravity-bending spatial arena. Inspired by games such as Manifold Garden and Rocket League.

This was developed as part of an undergraduate dissertation looking into accessibility for competitive online multiplayer games.

Key Achievements

Online Functionality

Researched the basic theory behind connecting players over the internet before looking into potential tools to facilitate implementation.

Chose Unity Gaming Services after discovering its ease of use, extensive documentation and community support.

Accessibility

Implemented accessibility options including controller support, button mapping, and text settings.

Gained valuable knowledge surrounding the importance of accessibility in games, the principles of human-computer interaction and the various testing methods to aid development.
